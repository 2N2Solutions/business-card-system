## Best Practices

### Profile Photo

For best results, use a photo that:
- Is professionally taken
- Shows your face clearly
- Has good lighting
- Is at least 400x400 pixels
- Uses a neutral background

### Sharing Tips

1. **Physical Cards**: Include your QR code on printed materials
2. **Networking**: Show the QR code directly from your phone
3. **Virtual Meetings**: Share your screen with the QR code
4. **Social Media**: Pin the link to your profiles

### Follow Up

When someone saves your contact:
1. They receive your complete info instantly
2. You appear in their contacts with your photo
3. They can easily find you later
4. Your social links are included

## Troubleshooting

### Card not loading?
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache

### QR code not scanning?
- Ensure adequate lighting
- Hold the phone steady
- Try increasing screen brightness
- Move closer/farther from the code

### Contact not saving?
- Allow the download in your browser
- Check your Downloads folder
- Import the .vcf file manually to Contacts
